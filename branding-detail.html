About Taco Del Sol

Taco Del Sol was never created to be the most authentic Mexican restaurant in Vietnam.

It was created to bring people together.

Inspired by the vibrant street food culture of Mexico, Taco Del Sol celebrates bold flavors, loud conversations, shared plates, and unforgettable nights.

From handmade tortillas and slow-cooked meats to fresh salsas and smoky grills, every dish is designed to be approachable, fun, and full of personality.

Whether you're here for tacos, margaritas, or simply good company, Taco Del Sol is a reminder that great food tastes even better when shared.

Because life is better under the sun.

About Founder

For more than ten years, I have been building restaurants. Not as a designer. Not as an agency owner. But as someone who lives with the consequences of every business decision.

I have opened restaurants. I have closed restaurants. I have lost money. I have rebuilt. I have managed construction sites, kitchens, service teams, marketing campaigns, menus, suppliers, customers, and everything in between.

Over the years, I realized something: Most businesses do not fail because of bad products. They fail because all the important pieces never connect. A beautiful brand without operations fails. A great restaurant without a clear business model struggles. A stunning interior without a customer experience system becomes expensive decoration. And marketing without a strong product is simply noise. That realization became the beginning of NOIR. NOIR was never created to be another design agency. It was built as a business-building studio. A place where strategy, branding, operations, customer experience, interiors, menus, media, and commercial thinking come together as one system. Because that is how real businesses are built. Today, through Sol Group and NOIR, I work with founders to create brands that are not only beautiful, but profitable, scalable, and meaningful. Brands that customers remember. Brands that teams believe in. Brands that can survive long after the opening day excitement disappears. Because in the end, we don't design brands. We build businesses.
